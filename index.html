Frequently asked questions

Regular AI notetakers only record and summarize after the meeting. Cluely works in real-time — giving you live answers, suggestions, and context while you're still in the conversation. It's the difference between a summary and a superpower.

Cluely is built for anyone who has meetings — sales reps, recruiters, founders, engineers, product managers, and more. Whether you're closing deals, interviewing candidates, or running team syncs, Cluely helps you perform better in every conversation.

Cluely offers a free tier that includes basic meeting notes and limited real-time assists. For unlimited meetings, advanced AI answers, follow-up emails, and team features, check out our Pro and Team plans on the Pricing page.

Cluely runs silently in the background — no bot joins your call, no "recording" notifications appear, and nothing shows up on other participants' screens. It captures audio directly from your system and screen context locally, so it's completely invisible to everyone else on the call.

Cluely supports over 30 languages for transcription and real-time assistance. It works with all major meeting platforms including Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Webex, and Slack Huddles — plus any browser-based conferencing tool.
